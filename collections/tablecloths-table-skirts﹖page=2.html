Tablecloths

 

Despite its self-explanatory name, a good linen table cloth is more than just a “mere piece of cloth” to cover the top of a table, it is the basis for any décor. But how do you wrap your head around such an immense variety of options that we have to offer, and make the right choice for your unique event?  Well, at efavormart, we always have just the perfect thing to cover your tables, no matter what kind of occasion you are planning.

Our fascinating collection of table linens includes everything you need to make a huge splash on your décor – from traditional satin tablecloths to modern-style options – all at wholesale prices. Want to add a jazzy vibe?  We’ll get you covered with our sparkling sequin tablecloths. Tired of ironing? Check out our “forget-about-wrinkles” spandex and fitted tablecloths. If you are planning an informal bash, don’t hesitate to take a look at our selection of polyester round tablecloths as well. What’s more, all our tabletop linens come in a variety of sizes, shapes, and colors to fit any type of table or theme.

If you don’t happen to find what you are looking for, which is highly unlikely, not to worry!  All our tablecloths can be easily customized with table runners, overlays, or linen napkins, to achieve the look that you want. Last but not least, all our tabletop linens are easy to maintain, which means you can use them more than once. In other words, they are definitely worth your time and attention, just see for yourself!
